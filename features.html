HydroMate Features

Discover all the amazing features that make HydroMate the smartest water bottle on the market

Smart App Integration

The HydroMate app connects seamlessly with your smart water bottle via Bluetooth. Track your daily water intake, set personalized hydration goals, and receive reminders to drink water throughout the day.

  • Real-time hydration tracking
  • Personalized hydration goals based on your body weight, activity level, and local weather
  • Weekly and monthly hydration reports
  • Integration with health apps like Apple Health and Google Fit

Advanced Temperature Control

HydroMate uses vacuum insulation technology to maintain your beverage's temperature for hours, whether hot or cold. The built-in temperature sensor displays the current temperature of your drink on the bottle's LED display.

  • Keeps cold drinks cold for up to 24 hours
  • Keeps hot drinks hot for up to 12 hours
  • Real-time temperature display
  • Temperature alerts when your drink reaches optimal drinking temperature

Intelligent Hydration Reminders

Never forget to drink water again. HydroMate's smart reminder system uses gentle glowing lights and optional vibrations to remind you when it's time to hydrate, based on your personal hydration schedule.

  • Customizable reminder intervals
  • Gentle LED glow reminders
  • Optional vibration alerts
  • Smart reminders that adapt to your drinking habits

Extended Battery Life

HydroMate's energy-efficient design ensures you won't have to charge your water bottle frequently. A single charge lasts up to 30 days with normal use, and the bottle can be easily recharged using the included USB-C cable.

  • 30-day battery life on a single charge
  • Fast charging - full charge in just 2 hours
  • USB-C charging port with water-resistant cover
  • Battery level indicator on bottle and in app

Premium Durable Design

Built to last, HydroMate is made from high-quality, BPA-free stainless steel that can withstand the rigors of daily use. The sleek, modern design is available in multiple colors to match your personal style.

  • Premium 18/8 food-grade stainless steel
  • BPA-free and phthalate-free materials
  • Leak-proof lid with secure lock
  • Scratch-resistant powder coating
  • Available in multiple stylish colors
