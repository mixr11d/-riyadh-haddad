Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?

The HydroMate battery life varies by model: Basic lasts up to 14 days, Pro lasts up to 21 days, and Elite lasts up to 30 days on a single charge with normal use. Battery life may vary based on usage patterns, especially if you use the LED reminder lights frequently.

Is the HydroMate dishwasher safe?

No, the HydroMate should not be placed in the dishwasher as it contains electronic components. We recommend hand washing with mild soap and water. The lid can be disassembled for thorough cleaning. Make sure to keep the charging port covered and dry.

How do I connect my HydroMate to the app?

Download the HydroMate app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Create an account and follow the in-app instructions to pair your bottle via Bluetooth. Make sure your bottle is charged and Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. The app will guide you through the setup process, which takes less than 2 minutes.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your HydroMate, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The product must be in its original condition with all accessories. Please contact our customer support team to initiate a return.

Does HydroMate ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can see the shipping options and costs during checkout. Please note that customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes that may apply in their country.
